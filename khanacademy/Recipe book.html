Grandma's Pie

  • Time: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Serves: 6-8
Ingredients Quantity
Apples4
Sugar1/2 cup
Cinnamon1 tsp
Pie crust1
Butter2 tbsp
Flour1 tbsp

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
Step 2: Peel and slice apples.
Step 3: Mix apples with sugar, cinnamon, and flour.
Step 4: Place mixture in pie crust.
Step 5: Dot with butter and cover with top crust.
Step 6: Bake for 50 minutes until golden brown.

Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour1 cup
Milk1 cup
Eggs2
Sugar2 tbsp
Baking powder1 tsp
Butter2 tbsp
Maple syrupTo serve

Step 1: Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
Step 2: Whisk in milk and eggs until smooth.
Step 3: Heat a pan and melt butter.
Step 4: Pour batter into pan and cook until bubbles appear.
Step 5: Flip and cook other side until golden.
Step 6: Serve with maple syrup.

Source: Fam's kitchen
